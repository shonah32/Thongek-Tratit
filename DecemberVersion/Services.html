CORPORATE REGISTRATION / AUTHORITY LIAISON

We provide liaison services with the Revenue Department in various matters including requesting for document copy, defend tax issues, tax filing, etc.

Social Security & Labour Office

We provide advice in relation to the social security filing and liaison services with the labour office to initiate/defend labour dispute, as well as to follow up with the related matters and suggest the finest solution.

We deals with other authorities to address your matters, including the district office, Office of The Attorney General, Ombudsman, Civil Aviation Authority of Thailand, Stock Exchange of Thailand, Consumer Protection Committee, and other government authorities / state enterprises.

VISA & WORK PERMIT

We provide service to ensure the client’s full compliance in visa and work permit matters.

We provide advice before the expats would enter into the country until their work permits are issued. We also provide services to renew their visa and perform 90-day report.

Additionally, we provide service for pilots and other careers which require special permission from the Labour Department to obtain their work permit.

BOI APPLICATION

We lead our client to the correct channel of BOI application (Board of Investment) and provide consultation in relation to exercising BOI privileges. We also provide advice about the planning for BOI project, including the machinery import, expat employment, producing annual financial statements.

FOREIGN BUSINESS LICENSE

We provide advice and apply foreign business license to the foreign company client. We also provide clear information about the timeline and possibility of the application, as well as the post-obligations of the client to comply with and perform the conditions of the license.
