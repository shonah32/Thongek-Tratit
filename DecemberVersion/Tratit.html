TRATIT PHONGORDETE

LL.B. Chulalongkorn University, LL.M. University of Minnesota

He started his career as a paralegal and gained experience in every area of legal practice, including but not limited to contract drafting, legal consultancy, litigation, immigration law, labour law. He also holds the notary public license which enable him to notarize the documents in compliance with the requirements of the Lawyer Council of Thailand.
