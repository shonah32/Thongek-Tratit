OUR HISTORY

Established in 2017, Thong-ek and his son, Tratit, set up a new legal consultancy and services company, namely Thong-ek and Tratit Co., Ltd. using the trade name as “Thong-ek & Tratit Law Office”.

Thong-ek is well-known among major clients in Thailand as one of the leading Thai legal advisors.

Tratit, Thong-ek’s only son, is also considered as a reliable and creative legal advisor who can efficiently blend the law and business into an effective solution for clients.

Nathara, a daughter of Tratit, will be the third generation who would join the firm soonest after her graduation.
